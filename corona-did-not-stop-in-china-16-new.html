The sequence of Coronavirus infection in China does not seem to stop. On Tuesday, 16 new cases of coronavirus were reported in China. Now the number of corona infected patients has increased to 82,881 in China. China's health officials gave this information on Tuesday in Beijing.

China's National Health Commission (NHC) said that a case came to light in Shanghai on Monday. The infected man had returned to China from abroad.
